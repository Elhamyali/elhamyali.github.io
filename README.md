### Web scraping and exploratory data analysis for HIV edutainment comic

Faced with the challenge of reaching 700k+ Webtoons comic readers, the New York State Department of Health AIDS Institute needed a more centralized and interactive platform where readers could subscribe to edutainment episodes, access actionable HIV resources, and interact with a community. I redesigned the [YGetIt?](https://www.ygetit.org/) website using concept and usability testing, and launched evidence-based social media campaigns. Alongside another team member, I used Python, specifically the Pandas and Selenium libraries to scrape public comments from the [Tested](https://www.webtoons.com/en/canvas/tested/list?title_no=231173&page=1) comic on Webtoons. The output was a CSV which I used to do exploratory data analysis to understand summary statistics. I completed a thematic analysis of the qualitative data to provide content development insights for future episodes. This led to continued $1.6M grant funding and increased engagement with the website, contributing to more equitable health education in New York.
